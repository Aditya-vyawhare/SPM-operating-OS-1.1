# Skill: Experimentation Framework (GMV and Monetization)

Use this skill to design and evaluate A/B tests for pricing, UX, policy flows, and upsells.

## Experiment design checklist

- Clear hypothesis tied to GMV lever
- Primary metric and guardrails defined
- Segment and eligibility rules documented
- Sample size and runtime estimated
- Rollout and rollback criteria agreed

## Common experiment types

## 1) Pricing experiments

- Service fee presentation
- Ancillary bundle pricing
- Markup strategy by route/company tier

Primary metrics:

- conversion rate, AOV, take rate, cancellation rate

## 2) UI and funnel experiments

- Checkout simplification
- Policy visibility changes
- Fare confidence messaging

Primary metrics:

- stage conversion, payment success, booking completion time

## 3) Upsell experiments

- Seat/meal/baggage prompts
- Bundled vs standalone ancillary offers

Primary metrics:

- attach rate, ancillary GMV, refund rate

## Analysis standard

- Report absolute and relative lift
- Segment impact by mode and customer type
- Include confidence level and risk notes
- Decide: ship, iterate, or stop

## Output template

1. Hypothesis and expected GMV impact
2. Experiment design
3. Results summary
4. Decision and next step
